Welcome to Marwadi University

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution fostering academic excellence, innovation, and ethical leadership, shaping socially responsible professionals.


Our Mission

To provide quality education through advanced curricula and experiential learning. To promote research, development, and innovation in core areas. To cultivate strong ethical values and social commitment among students.


Program/Course Outcomes (COs)

CO 1: Knowledge Application

Students will be able to apply fundamental knowledge of mathematics, science, and engineering to solve complex problems.

CO 2: Problem Analysis

Ability to identify, formulate, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions.

CO 3: Design/Development

Capability to design solutions for complex engineering problems and design system components or processes.

CO 4: Ethics and Society

Understand the impact of engineering solutions in societal and environmental contexts, and demonstrate knowledge of professional ethics.
